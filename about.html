About Us

SwagBox is a company that specializes in designing and manufacturing water level controller systems. Their systems are designed to help improve the efficiency of water storage in overhead tanks, with a wide range of system types and features to provide the ideal solution for various applications. SwagBox's water level controller systems feature advanced technology, durability, and reliability, making them easy to install and operate. They are suitable for various applications, including residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural sectors. With exceptional customer support and a team of experienced professionals, SwagBox has become a reliable partner for customers seeking water level control solutions.
